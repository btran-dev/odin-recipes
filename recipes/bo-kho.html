Recipe for Bo Kho

Description

Bo Kho is a Vietnamese beef stew seasoned with traditional fragrants such as lemongrass, star anime, and cinnamon. The stew is pot-roasted, with beef that will fall apart and soft carrots to compliment the beef. Turnips, daikon radish, and potatoes are often added. It is typically served with rice, rice noodles, or a freshly baked baguette.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp. brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp. ginger (grated)
  • 2 tsp. Chinese 5-spice powder
  • 1/2 tsp. black pepper
  • 3 lb. beef chuck, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 tbsp. vegatable oil
  • 6 large shallows or 1 medium red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup chopped tomato, fresh or canned
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 tbsp. finely chopped lemongrass, tender center only
  • 1 tsp. koser salt
  • 1/4 tsp. annatto powder (optional)
  • 4 star anise pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick (2-inches), or substituted cassia bark
  • 1-2 Serrano/Thai chiles, stem on, split lengthwise
  • 1 1/2 pounds medium carrots, peeled, cut int 2-inch chunks
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced scallions

Steps

  1. Make the marinade: stir together fish sauce, sugar, ginger, 5-spice powder and pepper.
  2. Put beef in large bowl, add marinade and massage meat. Marinate for at least 15 minutes.
  3. Put oil in Dutch oven over medium-high heat. When oil is hot, fry the beef cubes in small batches until nicely browned. When all beef is browned, return chunks to put. Add shallots, stir to combine and continue cooking for 4-5 minutes, or until softened.
  4. Add tomato, ginger, garlic, lemongrass, salt, and annatto (optional) and stir well to coat. Then, add star anise, cinnamon and chile. Cover with 4 cups water and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat to a simmer and cover with a lid. Cook for about 1 hour 15 minutes, or until the beef is fork-tender.
  6. Add carrots to the pot and cook for another 15 minutes. Skim fat from surface of the broth as necessary. Alternatively, refrigerate overnight and remove congealed fat before reheating.
  7. Ladle into individual bowls to serve. Garnish with scallions, cilantro, mint and basil
